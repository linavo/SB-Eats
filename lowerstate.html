Lower State Street

Lower State Street in Santa Barbara maybe arguably be the most popular area for tourists to visit. From shopping malls to a plethora of restaurants and bars, you can't go wrong with exploring here! Since the pandemic in 2020, Lower State Street as remained closed to drivers to commute back and forth. Instead, the street is now used to allow restaurants to provide outdoor seating for visitors to take advantage of the great weather.

There are plenty of restaurants that range from brunch to late night lounges. For visitors who prefer to enjoy being out in the sun, there are plenty of restaurants that provide unlimited mimosas or nice hot coffee in the morning. For those who enjoy nightlife as their main itinerary of the day, there are plenty of bars and clubs to visit while the night is young. Lower State Street is near the wharf where many visitors like to go to have a great view of the ocean.
