Lower State Street

Lower State Street might be considered the heart of Santa Barbara to many. Due to the pandemic, State Street has transformed into a closed off area for travelers to go as they please! Many of the restaurants that are located on the street can now utilize the extra space for nice outdoor dining. Santa Barbara's weather is unmatched, and Lower State Street is a must visit area to go to for tourists. The area is filled with tons of shopping areas to go to after dining at one of the many restaurants here! The now re-opened State Street allows visitors to even bring their pets to sit outside for comfortable seating.

Hibachi Steak House & Sushi Bar

The previously known restaurant Something's Fishy has now re-branded into Hibachi Steak House & Sushi Bar! The popular restaurant is known for providing entertainment while cooking up their food. The hibachi grill ranges from meats with rice to a whole menu for different rolls. There are plenty of options for those who are in the mood for fresh fish or cooked meats!

Holdren's Steaks & Seafood

Holdren's Steaks & Seafood is a classic steakhouse for fancy dinner nights. The menu ranges from different cuts of steak to lobster. Their menu and service will make you not regret making reservations ahead!

Pascucci

Pascucci provides their customers with a great ambience and delicious pasta! There are plenty of items on the menu that ranges from antipasta to some of the greatest ravioli on State Street. They have a variety of different wines and beers to compliment the Italian menu.
